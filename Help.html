How to Use Our Website

To use our shop, first sign in with your Google account. Once signed in, you can access the shop by clicking the "Bring me to shop" button located at the top right corner of the webpage. The helper provides tools and resources to guide you through your purchases. You can browse shirts, check to-do lists, and access important documents. Make sure to explore all features to get the most out of your experience!
